HELP!

  1. How can I register on Edustream?
    To register on Edustream, go to the registration page and provide the requested information, such as name, email, and password. Follow the instructions to complete the registration.

  2. How can I access my account on Edustream?
    To access your account on Edustream, go to the login page and enter your credentials, which are your email and password used during registration.

  3. Can I recover the password if I forget it?
    Yes, if you forget your password, go to the password recovery page and follow the instructions to reset it. You will receive an email with the necessary instructions.

  4. How do I find notes for a specific subject?
    You can search for notes using the search bar on the main page. Enter the name of the subject you're interested in, and related notes will be displayed.

  5. Can I take courses on Edustream?
    Yes, you can take courses on Edustream. Navigate to the "Courses" section and find the course you're interested in. Click on the course to access its content and start following it.

  6. Can I download notes or teaching materials?

  7. How can I contact Edustream customer support?
    If you have questions or issues, you can contact Edustream customer support through the contacts section on the right menu. Provide details of your issue, and the support team will assist you as soon as possible.

  8. Can I contribute my own notes or teaching materials?
    Yes, but only verified people. If you want to give materials through the platform, please contact us by using the contacts section on the right menu.

  9. Are there any costs associated with using Edustream?
    You can register an account for free, but you must pay the material before using.
